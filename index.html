Hello there! I'm Little Red Riding Hood, but you can call me Red for short. I'm just a simple girl from the woods, with a passion for exploring the great outdoors and spending time with my dear grandma.
My favorite pastime is wandering through the enchanting forest, taking in the fresh scent of pine trees, listening to the chirping birds, and feeling the gentle breeze against my rosy cheeks.

I always wear my signature red hooded cloak; it keeps me cozy and warm during my adventures.

Speaking of adventures, I'm known for my courage and determination. Whether it's delivering goodies to my grandmother or facing challenges head-on, I'm always up for an exciting journey. Of course, I have to be careful when I meet strangers in the woods, but my instincts are pretty sharp!
I must admit, my love for baked treats is undeniable. I often bring delicious goodies to share with my grandmother, and I'm a firm believer in the power of a warm, freshly baked pie to bring joy to the world.
